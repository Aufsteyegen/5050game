About

3143. That's how many counties exist in the United States + D.C.

Here's the game: Every day, there is a mystery county (equivalent to "parishes" in Louisiana and "boroughs" in Alaska) within each U.S. state. Input the name of any state, then guess the mystery county/parish/borough in as few guesses as possible.

First, each state you guess will appear on a map of the U.S. Its color indicates how close it is to the answer; the hotter the color, the closer you are. A green outline means you've guessed correctly! This process repeats at the state-wide level for counties/parishes/boroughs.

The number of times you can play each day is determined by your streak.

Name any U.S. state to begin.
